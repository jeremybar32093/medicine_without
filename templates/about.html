About Us

MedicineWithout CT Scans. MedicineWithout MRI. MedicineWithout Labs.

MedicineWithout Fear.

This site is dedicated to all those who practice MedicineWithout: Doctors, Nurses, Medics, EMTs, Military personnel, Firefighters, Law Enforcement Officers, first responders, and YOU. YES, YOU! Because at some point in your life, you will be confronted with some form of medical emergency and will need to practice MedicineWithout.

At MedicineWithout, all of the resources posted here have been written, created by, and/or reviewed by medical professionals, first responders, wilderness professionals, and/or other professionals in their respective fields. All of the information posted here is accurate to the best of our knowledge. Links to appropriate references are provided where appropriate. As we are always looking to improve, we invite debate and constructive, well-intentioned criticism on all of our work. Fake news has no home here.

If you have feedback or want a specific topic featured, let us know! Or better yet, submit a piece of your own! We’ll review it, and if we like it, we’ll post it, giving you full credit.
