Founder and CEO

Dr. Jonathan Bar, MD grew up in Fair Lawn, NJ where he began is career in medicine by joining the local ambulance corps. at the age of 16. Dr. Bar went on to attend Cornell University where he continued his EMS career serving as Cornell's chief EMS operations officer and obtained a BS with Distinction in Human Biology, Health and Society, followed by his MD with Honors in Research for his work on topical combat hemostatics. Dr. Bar is currently serving as a Chief Resident in his final year of residency in Emergency Medicine at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania and its affiliates, including Penn Presbyterian Medical Center where Dr. Bar had a crucial role in the design of disaster incident command systems and in implementing specialized tents for screening patients for the novel coronavirus. Post residency, Dr. Bar will be starting fellowship in EMS at Cooper University Medical Center.

In his spare time, Dr. Bar enjoys hiking and other outdoor activities, studying military leadership and history, and grilling.
